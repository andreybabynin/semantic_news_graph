FAQ

This is an alpha-stage prototype of news-graph dashboard. Using it you can:

  • Find what and when was mentioned in Russian news telegrams.
  • Get better understanding of the current news narrative.
  • Explore how people, organizations and locations are interconnected.

    • How it works

  • By entering a named entity (like Москва), you can create a dynamic graph with the ability to zoom and drag.
  • By clicking on the graph edges, you can see under the graph box all the news that it was associated with
  • Depth parameter allows you to change the graph depth, finding more distant associations.
  • Min news threshold stands for minimum number of news in the period
